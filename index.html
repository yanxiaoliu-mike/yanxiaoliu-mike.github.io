About me

Before I join The Chinese University of Hong Kong, I received my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Information Engineering and minor degree in Philosophy from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) in 2021. I was a visiting researcher in Stanford University, ISL Lab, hosted by Professor Ayfer Özgür and supported by PhD IMPAC Award 2023–24, from January 2024 to June 2024. I will graduate in summer 2025 and I am looking for postdoc positions. My Erdős number is 4.
